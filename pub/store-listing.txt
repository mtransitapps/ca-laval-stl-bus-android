Full description:
This application adds Laval STL buses information to MonTransit.

The app contains the planned schedule as well as real time predictions and alerts from NextBus and the latest news from YouTube and @stlsynchro on Twitter.

STL serves Laval and Montreal in Quebec, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the Société de Transport de Laval.
http://www.stl.laval.qc.ca/en/stl-synchro/developers/

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-laval-stl-bus-android

This app is not related with the Société de Transport de Laval.

Permissions:
- Other: required for real-time schedule predictions & alert messages (NextBus) & to read news from YouTube and Twitter.
